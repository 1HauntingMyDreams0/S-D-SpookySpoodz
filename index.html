Beyond the Ordinary

Hi, I’m Destinee — a SAHM who found an unexpected passion in the tiniest, most curious moment. During a simple garage sale, a little jumping spider crossed my path, and something just clicked.

That spark turned into late-night research, new enclosures, and eventually my husband and I welcoming more spiders into our home.

Not long after, a friend’s spider had babies, and we took them in with open arms. Suddenly, we weren’t just hobbyists — we were caretakers of a growing collection of slings, each one teaching us something new and deepening our love for the hobby.

What began as a chance encounter has grown into a family project and a dream. Now, I’m building a small business focused on creating thoughtful, quality enclosures and offering healthy, well-raised spider babies to others who share this fascination.

It’s been an unexpected journey, but one that’s brought so much joy, purpose, and connection into our lives — and I’m excited to share that with you.
