Beyond the Ordinary

Hi, I’m Destinee — a SAHM who found an unexpected passion in the tiniest, most curious moment. During a simple garage sale, a little jumping spider crossed my path, and something just clicked.

That spark turned into research, new enclosures, and eventually caring for a growing collection of slings — each one deepening our passion for the hobby.

What began as a chance encounter has grown into a family project and a dream. We now focus on raising healthy spiders and creating quality enclosures.

It’s been an unexpected journey, and we’re excited to share it with you.
