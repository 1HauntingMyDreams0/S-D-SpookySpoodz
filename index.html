Our Story — From One Spider to a Growing Passion

Hi, I’m Destinee — a stay-at-home mom who discovered a passion for jumping spiders in the most unexpected way. What started as a simple moment turned into a deep love for raising and caring for these incredible little creatures.

Today, we raise healthy, well-cared-for pet jumping spiders and create custom enclosures designed to keep them safe, happy, and thriving.

Every spider we offer is raised with care, attention, and respect — because to us, they’re more than pets, they’re part of the family.
